Your Voice Matters in Shaping Toronto

Public Square TO provides access to tools and resources that help you engage with local government, participate in public consultations, and make your voice heard on issues that affect your community.

Civic Engagement Tools

City Consultations

Find and participate in official public consultations from the City of Toronto. Share your feedback on upcoming policies, development projects, and community initiatives.

Get Involved

Council Tracker

Track City Council meetings, motions, and votes. Learn how your local representatives are voting on issues that matter to you and your neighborhood.

Track Decisions

Neighborhood Forums

Connect with neighbors to discuss local issues, organize community initiatives, and collaborate on solutions to challenges facing your area of Toronto.

Join Discussions

Budget Feedback

Review Toronto's budget proposals and provide your input on spending priorities. Help shape how city resources are allocated in your community.

Share Priorities

Service Requests

Report issues in your neighborhood, request city services, and track the status of your requests. Help keep Toronto's neighborhoods clean, safe, and well-maintained.

Report Issues

Community Initiatives

Discover grassroots initiatives and volunteer opportunities in your community. Connect with local organizations working to improve Toronto neighborhoods.
